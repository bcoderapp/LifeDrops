Why Donate Blood?

Blood donation is a simple act of humanity that can save lives. Here’s why you should consider donating blood:

  • Every two seconds, someone in need of a transfusion requires blood.
  • Your donation can help accident victims, cancer patients, and more.
  • It's a quick and painless process that makes a huge difference.

FAQs

Q: How often can I donate blood? A: You can donate whole blood every 56 days.

Q: Is it safe to donate blood? A: Yes, the process is completely safe and conducted under medical supervision.
